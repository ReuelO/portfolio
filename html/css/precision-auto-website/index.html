About Precision Auto

Founded in 2005, Precision Auto has been serving the community with dedication and expertise. What started as a small garage has grown into a trusted name in auto repair, built on principles of honesty, quality workmanship, and exceptional customer service.

We continually invest in the latest diagnostic tools and training for our technicians to ensure we can handle any automotive challenge, from routine maintenance to complex engine repairs. Our goal is to provide reliable, affordable, and honest service, ensuring your vehicle is safe and performing at its best.
