About Isabella

Isabella Rossi is a passionate fashion designer with over a decade of experience in creating bespoke garments that blend classic elegance with contemporary flair. Her journey began at the prestigious Central Saint Martins, where she honed her skills in couture and textile innovation.

With a keen eye for detail and a deep understanding of form and movement, Isabella believes that clothing should not only look beautiful but also make the wearer feel empowered and confident. Her designs often feature intricate hand embroidery, luxurious fabrics, and unique silhouettes that celebrate individuality.

Isabella's work has been featured in various fashion editorials and showcased at exclusive events, earning her recognition for her distinct aesthetic and commitment to craftsmanship.
