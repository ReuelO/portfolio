About Soko Bora Supplies

At Soko Bora Supplies, we are passionate about supporting the agricultural community across Kenya. Founded with the vision of providing accessible, high-quality farm inputs, we've grown to be a trusted name among small-scale and commercial farmers alike.

We believe that a thriving agricultural sector is the backbone of our economy. That's why we meticulously source and supply a wide range of seeds, fertilizers, pesticides, and farming tools, ensuring every product meets rigorous quality standards. Our commitment extends beyond sales; we aim to be a resource for knowledge and sustainable farming practices.
